About Me.

Hi, I'm Syam Krishna S, an Interior Designer based in Kozhikode with a strong foundation in civil engineering. I hold a diploma in Civil Engineering, which gives me a unique edge in blending structural insight with creative interior solutions.

My design approach is rooted in functionality and aesthetic balance, ensuring every space is both visually appealing and practically designed. I take pride in creating interiors that reflect the personality and needs of my clients, while ensuring quality and technical accuracy.

Whether it's a residential home, a commercial space, or a renovation project, I bring a deep understanding of materials, structure, and design harmony to every project I take on
